Q1: Why might we consider using Git? 

Answer: Git is incredibly helpful for collaboration, specifically allowing developers to collaborate on a project simultaneously 
while maintaining a complete history of all changes made to the codebase. Thus, I might consider using Git if I need to collaborate on a project. 

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example. 

Answer: Data science could be applied to the medical field. Specifically, it could be used to analyze patient data to identify risk factors,
predict disease progression, and recommend personalized treatments.
